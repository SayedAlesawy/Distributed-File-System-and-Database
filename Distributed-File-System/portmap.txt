1 - Request Ports (Trackers listen to client requests here, and clients use them to send requests to trackers) ==> {9001, 9002, ...}

2 - Normal Trackers (ports on which trackers listen to normal Data Nodes) ==> {8001, 8002, ...}

3 - Heartbeat port (Used by heart beat data nodes to send their IPs to the heartbeat tracker process) ==> {9092}

4 - Heartbeat DataNode ports ==> {3001, 3002, ...}

5 - Normal DataNode ports ==> {4001, 4002, ...}

6 - Client ports ==> {5001, 5002, ...}
